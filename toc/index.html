Toc is a proof-of-concept decentralized messaging app.

Toc aims to redefine the expectations of what a decentralized app can do.

Most decentralized apps lock your data to a single device. With Toc, your contacts, messages, and customizations can be available anywhere you use it.

A lot of effort has been put into making Toc easy to use, but you probably shouldn't use it for anything important yet, because it may still have serious bugs and security issues.

Toc is completely free and open-source, and is available on Android, iOS, and right here on your browser.
