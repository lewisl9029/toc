Toc is a proof-of-concept distributed messaging app.

Toc aims to redefine the expectations of what a distributed app can do.

Most distributed apps limit your data to a single device. With Toc, your contacts, messages, and customizations can be available anywhere.

A lot of effort has been put into making Toc easy to use, but you probably shouldn't use it for anything important. There are known issues with regards to functionality and security that may never get resolved.

For those looking for more reliable alternatives to Toc, we recommend taking a look at other, more mature open-source projects like Tox and Matrix.

Toc is completely free and open-source, and is available on Android, iOS, and right here on your browser.
